# Otto Margin Desk demo video script
Target length: 2:05-2:30.

## 0:00-0:15 — Hook
Every restaurant margin leader has the same morning problem: prices move, freight moves, social narratives move, and margin decisions lag. Otto Margin Desk turns that into a tiny autonomous business.

## 0:15-0:40 — MoA council
This is not a single chatbot. Hermes MoA runs a council: GPT-5.5 as the first reference, GLM 5.2 Cloud as the second reference, and GPT-5.5 as the aggregator.

## 0:40-1:20 — Live loop
One cycle fetches public economic data, adds X/social market context, synthesizes the paid brief, and renders the customer-facing page.

## 1:20-1:50 — Business operations
A $3 checkout event creates revenue. The treasury ledger debits MoA synthesis, search, and hosting costs under safety limits. It reinvests into the next scouting cycle and prepares buyer delivery.

## 1:50-2:20 — Why it matters
It is useful because restaurant margin leaders pay for concise margin intelligence. It is viable because it uses Hermes primitives: MoA, scheduled runs, public data, payment events, ledger controls, and gateway delivery.

## 2:20-2:30 — Close
Otto Margin Desk is a repeatable template for autonomous business operations. Swap the signal pack and the same agent can run margin desks for restaurants, freight, SaaS, or local services.
