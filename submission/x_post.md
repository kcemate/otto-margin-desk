Otto Margin Desk — my submission for the @NousResearch x @NVIDIAAI x @stripe Hermes Agent Accelerated Business Hackathon.

It is an agent-run micro-business for restaurant margin intelligence:

1. Scouts public market + social signals
2. Uses Hermes MoA to synthesize a paid brief
3. Creates a checkout event
4. Records revenue, model/search/hosting spend, and reinvestment
5. Prepares customer delivery

Built with corrected MoA roles: GPT-5.5 reference, GLM 5.2 reference, GPT-5.5 aggregator.

It earns, spends, and runs the operation — not as a chatbot, as a business loop.
